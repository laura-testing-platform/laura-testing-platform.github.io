Why do I focus on career and wellness coaching? The first one might be an easy assumption, having worked in HR it is an area I have a lot of knowledge and experience in, but having also not known where I want to be ‘long-term’ and so many friends hitting the ‘career rut’ – it was a no-brainer! Aside from that, I love being a coach and I want to help people find what they love to do too.


And wellness? Well, having come close to burnout in 2016, despite thinking I had a grip on my work-life balance and being reasonably healthy, I’ve realised that it’s so easy to mis-calculate your own expectations of yourself. That’s why for me it is so important to make sure everyone knows and understands their own definition of balance and wellness. This can mean a big shift, or a small one, depending on you and what you want. I’m still experimenting with mine, and it’s a journey I love every day. For me, my happy place is when I am out on an early morning run.
