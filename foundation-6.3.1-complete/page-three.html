My Story and Contact

How did I get here?

After school, university, a masters, and the best part of ten years working in HR I was introduced to coaching. At the time, I was working in a HR management role in advertising (which I still do part-time). I did some research and attended a weekend conference in London where I had my ‘aha’ moment. I signed up to do two diplomas (Personal Performance, and Corporate and Executive) and less than a year later I qualified!

My coaching style

As a person I am very driven, honest, and I am known to like a plan! Although I am described as quite measured, my approach is tailored to each person. I see coaching as working in partnership, you don’t need to work on your goals alone. I will support you, remind you of your progress and celebrate your success.

Is this sounding a bit too positive? What about, you know, life? I understand that everyone has other things going on, so we will balance the realities of the day-to-day, so that you can remain focused on your journey. As long as you are 100% committed to the coaching process, good things will happen. Together we will help you find fulfilment and be the best version of yourself.

Who do I like to work with? Over the last ten years working in HR and coaching I have worked in retail, advertising and executive search with many different people. My coaching experience lies at middle and senior management level as well as 1-2-1 private clients of differing ages, careers, and goals.
